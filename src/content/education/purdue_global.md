The Master of Science in Information Technology program at Purdue University Global is a fully online graduate degree designed for working IT professionals advancing into senior technical and leadership roles. The curriculum bridges technology, communications, and business across fifteen courses spanning system analysis and design, information systems project management, database design, and applied statistics. The Data Analytics concentration focuses the elective track on statistical methods, analytics tooling, and foundational programming applied in an analytics setting.
<br/><br/>
This program is sponsored through ManTech as part of its commitment to professional development for delivery leaders. The MSIT complements an existing MBA by adding technical depth in database systems, applied statistics, and analytics programming, areas that business graduate coursework does not cover.
